I'm Matt, currently learning Software Engineering from London


I've always had a passion for logic puzzles and alternate ways to solve problems. I started playing about with Python in 2020 (during the lockdowns) while working full time as a Class 1 HGV Driver and had that "this is it" moment.

I started to complete challenges on TryHackMe, learning the control and flow of using Linux (including booting an old laptop to Kali) and how computer networking works. After a few weeks of this, I realised my passion was more aligned to bringing applications to life.

After the world started to return to normal and the presence of hybrid working became prominent, I decided that I needed to commit fully to a new career and start down the path of software engineering.

I took the decision to enrol at the University of London on a BSc Computer Science degree, resulting in a first semester score of 96 (Intro to Programming), confirmation that I'd chosen the right path!

Since that point, I have also completed a Software Engineering Bootcamp between Nov 22 and Feb 23, achieving a score of 100% overall.

I'm currently cementing my knowledge by building projects in Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript and getting familiar with frameworks such as Django.

Check out my projects page to see what I've been up to!
